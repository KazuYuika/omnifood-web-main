How it works

Order your customized cake in simple 3 steps

01

Choose your cake

Browse through our wide selection of customized cakes, including Valentine's Day cakes, birthday cakes, anniversary cakes, christening cakes, wedding cakes, gender reveal cakes, money pulling cakes, tiered cakes, debut cakes, bento cakes, bento combos, minimalist cakes, and cupcakes.

02

Customize your cake

Tell us what you want in your cake, including the flavor, design, and any special requests you may have. We can also create custom cakes based on your photos or ideas.

03

Place your order

Once you are happy with your cake design, simply place your order and we will take care of the rest. We offer a variety of payment options, including Gcash, cash, and 50% downpayment. You can pick up your order at our store in Bayambang, Pangasinan, or we can deliver it to you (with delivery charge) within the following areas: Bayambang, Alcala, Malasiqui, Bautista, Basista, Urbiztondo, Camiling, San Carlos, and Mangatarem.
